About The Hackathon

Amrita Online Hackathon 2026 is a national-level innovation challenge that empowers students to build impactful technology solutions for real-world problems.

What is Amrita Online Hackathon?

This hackathon brings together students, developers, designers, and innovators from across India to solve real-world challenges using technology.

Participants will collaborate for 72 hours, attend mentor sessions, learn from industry experts, and compete for exciting prizes.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, this event provides an opportunity to learn, innovate and showcase your talent.
